Chocolate & Peanut Butter Fudge

Fudge with flavours of chocolate & peanut butter.

A fudge with the sweetness of chocolate and health benefits of peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Sweetened condensed milk

Procedure

  1. In a medium bowl, combine chocolate chips and condensed milk.
  2. Microwave for 1 minute and mix together until smooth.
  3. Lay a piece of parchment paper over a 9x9-inch (23x23-cm) baking dish.
  4. Transfer chocolate mix to baking dish, spread evenly.
  5. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.
  6. Spread peanut butter evenly over the top of the cooled fudge.
  7. Refrigerate for 2 additional hours.
  8. Carefully cut into squares & Enjoy.
