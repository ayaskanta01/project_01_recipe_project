Dulche de Leche Swirl Fudge

Fudge with flavours of dulche de leche.

A fudge with the sweetness & flavours of argentenian sweet dulche de leche

Ingredients

  • White chocolate
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Dulce de leche

Procedure

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chocolate with sweetened condensed milk .
  2. Then melt in the microwave in 15-second increments.
  3. Pour chocolate mixture into a parchment paper-lined 20x20cm baking tin.
  4. Dollop your dulce de leche and make ribbons by swirling chocolate mixture.
  5. Chill for 2 hours or until the fudge is firm.
  6. Cut into bite-sized pieces. and Enjoy .
