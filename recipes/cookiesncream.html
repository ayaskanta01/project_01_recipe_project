Cookies 'n' Cream Fudge

Fudge with flavours of cream & cookies.

A fudge with the sweetness of white chocolate and the crunchiness of sandwich cookies

Ingredients

  • White chocolate
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Sandwich cookies, chopped

Procedure

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chocolate with sweetened condensed milk .
  2. Then melt in the microwave in 15-second increments.
  3. Once chocolate is fully melted, add in chopped cookies and stir until combined.
  4. Pour chocolate mixture into a parchment paper-lined 20x20cm baking tin.
  5. Chill for 2 hours or until the fudge is firm.
  6. Cut into bite-sized pieces. and Enjoy .
